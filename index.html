PyDiff holds monthly meetups in Cardiff for anyone who uses or wants to learn about Python. Everyone is welcome, from experienced programmers to new learners, wherever their interest lies.

We hold technical talks and workshops and occasional social events. We're also involved in a number of international Python/Django software community conferences - we've recently held:

  • Django Weekend Cardiff 2014
  • Python Nambia 2015
  • DjangoCon Europe 2015
and we were involved in PyCon Namibia 2016 in February, while PyCon UK will come to Cardiff in September 2016.
